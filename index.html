Plot Synopsis

SpongeBob is a square yellow sponge who lives in a pineapple in Bikini Bottom. His pet is a sea snail called Snail that can meow "miao~miao~". SpongeBob likes to catch jellyfish and is the top chef in The Krusty Krab. Patrick and Sandy are both his friends. SpongeBob always creates troubles for the peaceful world. Although he makes some jokes, he can always get out of trouble and then create new troubles.
